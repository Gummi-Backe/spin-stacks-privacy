1. Request account deletion

SpinStacks offers two ways to request permanent deletion. Both routes submit deletion of the same PlayFab player account and associated online data.

Inside the app

  1. Open SpinStacks and sign in.
  2. Tap your avatar to open the account area.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Tap it again within the confirmation period.

Without the app

Send a deletion request to SpinStacks support, preferably from the email address registered with the PlayFab account.

Request deletion by email r.kerner.developer@gmail.com

Include your PlayFab Account ID if you still know it. It is shown in the SpinStacks account area. Never include your SpinStacks password, Google password, payment-card details, or Google Play payment data.

2. Identity verification

Account ownership must be checked before an external deletion request is processed. Sending the request from the email address registered with the SpinStacks account is the preferred verification method. If you no longer have access to that address, contact support and explain the situation. Additional non-secret account details may be requested to prevent deletion of another player's account.

SpinStacks support will never ask for your password, Google sign-in credentials, or payment-card details.

3. Data removed

After ownership is verified, the deletion request is submitted to Microsoft PlayFab. The PlayFab master player account and associated PlayFab data are scheduled for deletion according to PlayFab's deletion process. This includes, where associated with the account:

  • the PlayFab player account, player ID, and account identifiers;
  • registered contact information and PlayFab login data;
  • the generated public alias and selected avatar information;
  • cloud progress, level, player data, and saved settings stored in PlayFab;
  • high scores, statistics, and leaderboard entries; and
  • multiplayer and service data covered by PlayFab's account deletion process.

Processing is asynchronous. The account cannot be recovered once PlayFab completes the deletion.

4. Data not removed by the PlayFab request

  • Local app data: Data stored only on your Android device must be removed by clearing SpinStacks app data or uninstalling the app.
  • Google Play purchase records: Purchases are managed separately by Google and are not deleted with the PlayFab account. Premium may be restored later with the same Google Play account if Google confirms the purchase.
  • Required records: Limited support, security, fraud-prevention, or legal records may be retained only where necessary and permitted by law. They are not used for advertising.

5. After deletion

Deletion is permanent. Signing in or reinstalling SpinStacks later may create a new PlayFab account with new progress and a new public alias. A previous high score, level, cloud progress, or leaderboard position cannot be restored after account deletion has completed.
